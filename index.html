MissileDefense

By Noe Jimenez

Your Mission:

You are the lone gunner. You must destroy the falling missiles before they reach you or the settlements.



Instructions:
  • To Fire: Click your mouse where you want your bullet to go

  • Detonation: Bullet will detonate when it arrives where you clicked

  • Destruction: Missiles hit by bullet, or within blast radius of exploded bullet, will be destroyed

  • Game Over: You are hit three times, or all settlements are destroyed
Press spacebar to begin. Good luck!
