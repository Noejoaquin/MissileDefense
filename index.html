MissileDefense

By Noe Jimenez

Welcome to MissileDefense! You are the lone gunner, defending your settlements from an arial attack. You must stop the falling missiles from reaching your post, as well as the innocents on either side of you. If you are hit three times, or all of your settlements are destroyed, the game is over.

To fire, click your mouse where you want your bullet to go. It will go to that location and detonate. Use your timing .

The missiles will fall as soon as you press the spacebar. Good luck!
