Missile Defense

Welcome to Missile Defense! You are the lone gunner, defending your settlements from an arial attack. You must stop the falling missiles from reaching your post, as well as the innocents on either side of you.

You have 20 shells per level.

Fire by clicking your mouse where you want your shell to go. It will go to that location, and detonate. Use your timing.

The missiles will fall as soon as you press the spacebar. Good luck!
